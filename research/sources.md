\## Why This Topic



AI-powered SEO content production is the most consequential shift in content marketing since Google's Panda update. The question is no longer whether to use AI for content — it's whether you're building systems that produce content that's genuinely better, or just faster.



Most practitioners are doing the former. The experts in this research are doing the latter.



This repository collects primary source material from 10 practitioners who are actively building and running AI content production systems — not commentators, not tool vendors, not people who write about AI SEO without doing it.



\---



\## Expert Selection Criteria



Every expert on this list was evaluated against three non-negotiable criteria:



\*\*1. Practitioner, not pundit\*\*

Have they actually built or run an AI content production system at a real company or agency? Experts who only write about AI SEO without demonstrable execution were excluded.



\*\*2. Active in 2025-2026\*\*

Are they publishing current thinking based on current experiments? Historical reputation without current activity was not sufficient.



\*\*3. Specific to AI content production\*\*

Does their work specifically address how AI changes the way content is researched, written, structured, distributed, and measured? General SEO experts who occasionally mention AI were excluded.

\## Key Themes Across All Sources



\*\*1. Quality over volume\*\*

Every expert agrees AI content fails when it scales bad content faster. The differentiator is editorial standards, not AI capability.



\*\*2. Off-property signals drive AI citations\*\*

Brand mentions, retrieval sources, and external document networks matter more than on-page optimization for AI visibility.



\*\*3. Training data timing is strategic\*\*

Publishing before a training cutoff has fundamentally different ROI than publishing after — an insight most content calendars ignore entirely.



\*\*4. Research is the bottleneck, not writing\*\*

Finding genuinely new information — through sub-agents, Reddit mining, proprietary data — is the hard part. AI handles the writing.



\*\*5. Measurement is broken\*\*

AI visibility metrics without business outcome tracking are misleading. Impressions up does not mean pipeline up.



\*\*6. Human judgment is the irreplaceable layer\*\*

AI can match patterns, generate drafts, and scale distribution. It cannot decide which pattern to break, which insight is genuinely new, or whether the content respects the reader's time. Every expert in this research uses AI as infrastructure while keeping human taste and judgment at the center of their production process.
